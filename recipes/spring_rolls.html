Wrappers

To prepare the wrappers, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 cup of All-Purpose Flour
  • 3 tablespoons of Cornstarch
  • 1.25 cups of Water, or as required to make a flowing batter with a slightly thin consistency
  • 0.25 tablespoon of Salt, or as preferred
  • Non-stick cooking oil, if necessary

Now that you have all of your ingredients ready (hopefully):

  1. Add all the dry ingredients into a bowl and whisk together.
  2. Slowly pour in the water while mixing until you get a thin, flowy consistency
  3. Heat a non-stick pan (or a normal pan greased with non-stick cooking oil) on low heat until it's just slightly hot
  4. Lift the pan from the stovetop and slowly pour in your batter; just enough to be large and thin but not too thin ya know
  5. Put the pan back on the stovetop and take out the wrapper when the sides start curling up and it becomes translucent
  6. Place the wrapper on a plate, cooked-side up (it's so thin that you didn't need to flip it to cook it through!)
  7. Repeat steps 4 through 6 for the rest of the batter
  8. Refridgerate until filling is done
  9. Congrats!
